The application aims to improve patient safety and treatment adherence by combining medication management
with intelligent mental health support in a safe and private environment.
Problem Statement
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
Forgetting medication doses
Incorrect drug usage
Dangerous drug interactions
Lack of continuous monitoring
Mental health struggles affecting adherence
Fear of seeking psychological help
Proposed Solution
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
Reminds patients of medications
Detects drug interactions
Tracks adherence
Provides mental support (without diagnosis)
Offers anonymous safe space for users
Uses AI to assist and guide users
Target Users
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
▪ 
Patients with chronic diseases
Elderly people
People with mental health struggles
Users who avoid visiting doctors
Caregivers (family members)

⚠️ Disclaimer: This application does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. It offers guidance,
monitoring, and support only. 
Screens Overview (85 Screens)
Authentication (8 Screens): Splash Screen, Onboarding (3 Screens), Login, Sign Up, OTP Verification, Forgot
Password
Profile Setup (6 Screens): Personal Information, Age & Gender, Chronic Diseases, Allergies, Emergency Contact,
Upload Medical Reports
Home & Dashboard (6 Screens): Home Dashboard, Daily Timeline, Upcoming Medications, Missed Doses, Quick
Actions, Risk Score
Medication Management (12 Screens): Add Medication, Medication Details, Dosage Setup, Frequency Setup,
Duration, Reminder Time, Medication List, Edit Medication, Delete Confirmation, Medication History, Refill Reminder,
Notes
Smart Reminder System (6 Screens): Reminder Notification, Snooze Reminder, Mark as Taken, Skip Dose,
Reminder Settings, Sound Settings
Drug Interaction (6 Screens): Interaction Checker, Scan Prescription (AI), Interaction Results, Risk Level, Warning
Alert, Suggested Alternatives
Reports & Analytics (5 Screens): Adherence Report, Weekly Report, Monthly Report, Health Charts, Export PDF
Caregiver & Doctor Module (5 Screens): Add Doctor, Share Data, Caregiver Access, Notifications to Caregiver,
Emergency Alert
Mental Health Module (15 Screens): Enter Anonymous Mode, Mental Dashboard, Daily Check-In, Mood Input,
Anxiety Level, Sleep Tracker, Journal Entry, AI Chat Support, Guided Conversation, Breathing Exercise, Meditation
Screen, Mood History, Mood Analysis, Emotional Insights, Crisis Alert Screen
Self-Assessment (5 Screens): Start Assessment, Questions Screen, Progress Indicator, Result (Mild / Moderate /
High), Guidance Screen
AI Features (5 Screens): AI Assistant, Voice Input, Smart Suggestions, Prediction Alerts, Behavior Analysis
Settings (4 Screens): Settings, Language, Notifications, Privacy & Security
UX Screens (2 Screens): Loading Screen, Success / Error Screen
